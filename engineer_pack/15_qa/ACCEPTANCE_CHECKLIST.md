# Acceptance Checklist

- All panels render with live data, not fake baked image text.
- All pipeline rows have ON/OFF, Run, Pause, Stop, Re-run.
- Resource monitor shows Vast GPU and Hostinger VPS accurately.
- Docker restart loops are visible and actionable.
- Inference Fabric shows tiers, model residency, blocked escalations, and why-decisions.
- Cross-Correlation daemon shows sources, jobs, conflicts, controls.
- Asset Forge generates image/GLB jobs, saves assets, and requires approval.
- Brain visualizer responds to voice, LLM events, alerts, and modes.
- Voice control can receive commands and speak updates.
- Critical actions require approval and are audit logged.
- Reduced-motion and safe-mode work.
- Performance remains usable on target hardware.
