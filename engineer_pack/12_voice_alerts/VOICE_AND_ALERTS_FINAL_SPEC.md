# Voice and Alerts Final Spec

- Voice Active microphone state.
- Speech input to command parser.
- Spoken alert output.
- Voice waveform visualizer.
- Speak, Mute, Listen, Broadcast Summary controls.
- Alert severities: info, success, warning, critical.
- Critical alerts interrupt with visible and audible warning unless muted.
- SFX must be short, clean, and optional.
