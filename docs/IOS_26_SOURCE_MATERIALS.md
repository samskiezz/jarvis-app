## Excerpts (Apple iOS 26 PDF)

### Design / Liquid Glass
- "Combining the optical qualities of glass with a sense of fluidity, Liquid Glass forms the foundation of the new design. It reflects and refracts what's underneath it in real time, while dynamically transforming to help bring greater focus to your content."
- "Dynamic tools & navigation … fluidly morph as you need access to more tools or move through different views."
- "In-place alerts … expand from the button that initiates them making a deletion, confirmation, phone call or action quicker and easier to reach."
- "Dynamic tab bars … shrink to create more space as you scroll, and fluidly expand the moment you need them when you scroll up or tap on the tab bar."
- "Expanded context menus … expanded into a vertical context menu to make finding the commands you need even easier."
- "Liquid Glass magnifier loupe — Move your text cursor with precision using a new, more fluid Liquid Glass magnifier."

### Home Screen / Icons
- "Redesigned app icons — Crafted out of multiple layers of Liquid Glass, app icons get a fresh new look in light, dark and tinted styles."
- "Clear icons & widgets — Customize your app icons with a new, all-clear glass look."
- "Light-tinted icons & widgets — Personalize your app icons and widgets with a lighter, colored tint."

### Camera / Photos
- "Spatial scenes — When you move your iPhone, your photo comes to life with a new 3D effect. With advanced computer vision techniques running on the Neural Engine, a spatial scene is generated from your 2D photos."
- "Lens cleaning hint — Camera will detect if the lens is smudged and display a message recommending cleaning it for the clearest possible photos."
- "Updated tab design — Photos features separate tabs for the Library and Collections views, along with quick access to Search."

### Visual Intelligence
- "Access systemwide — Access visual intelligence in any app by pressing the same buttons you use to take a screenshot."
- "Search Google and third-party apps visually."
- "Ask ChatGPT about what's currently on your iPhone screen."
- "Identify objects … plants, animals, and landmarks."
- "Add events to your calendar from a flyer on your iPhone screen."
- "Highlight to search by scrubbing over it with your finger."
- "Summarize, translate, or read text out loud."

### Live Translation
- "Automatically translate incoming texts, including group messages. Type your response in your preferred language, and it will be delivered to your friend in both the original and translated languages."
- "Audio translation in Phone — Translate a phone call you make or receive, using the More menu during a call."
- "Translated captions in FaceTime."
- "Hear translations for nearby conversations using AirPods."

### Genmoji / Image Playground
- "Combine emoji to make new Genmoji … mix together emoji to create something new."
- "Modify personal attributes — Change things like hairstyle and facial hair for the Genmoji that are inspired by people from your Photos library."
- "Add expressions — Select the perfect expression for your images of people."
- "More styles with ChatGPT … Watercolor and Oil Painting."
- "Genmoji in Image Playground."
- "Integration with Messages Backgrounds — Create images for your Messages Backgrounds using Image Playground."

### Phone
- "Unified layout — Choose a new unified layout that brings together your Favorites, Recents, and Voicemails."
- "Hold Assist — Keep your spot in line while you wait for a live agent. Once a live agent becomes available, the Phone app rings you to return to the call."
- "Call Screening — Screen incoming calls from unknown numbers. Your phone will pick up the call for you and ask for a name and reason for the call."
- "Unknown Callers list — Filter calls from unknown numbers in a new area of your call list."
- "SharePlay on a phone call — Watch, listen, and play together on phone calls."
- "Voicemail summaries — Apple Intelligence powers new voicemail summaries that appear inline with your missed calls."
- "Live Reply — Respond to a contact from the incoming call screen. Your response is spoken to the caller."
- "More button — Tap the new More button to quickly access … Call Recording, Live Translation, and SharePlay."

### Messages
- "Backgrounds — Make your conversations feel more personal with Backgrounds for everyone in the conversation to enjoy. Select from dynamic built-in options like water or aurora, choose any of your own photos, or create one with Image Playground."
- "Polls — Send polls to friends and family over iMessage … Apple Intelligence can even detect when a poll might come in handy and suggest one."
- "Text selection — Select portions of a text in a single bubble to copy and share."
- "Typing indicators in groups — Know exactly who is about to chime in with typing indicators in group chats."
- "Screen unknown senders — Screened messages from unknown numbers are placed in a new area of your conversation list."
- "On-device spam protection — On-device spam detection filters out spam from your conversations list."
- "Text Filters — sorting them into Promotions (offers, marketing, subscriptions) and Transactions (receipts, bills, confirmations)."
- "Allow Notifications — Select which type of messages … from unknown senders you want to be notified for."

### Maps
- "Preferred Routes — iPhone helps improve your daily routine by using on-device intelligence to learn and understand the routes you take between places you frequently visit."
- "Natural Language Search — Search the way you talk with support for questions like 'find cafes with free Wi-Fi'."
- "New incident report types — report and view road closures, traffic, and road work."
- "Visited Places — You can choose to allow your iPhone to intelligently detect the places you visit … view or search for places you've visited in the Maps places library."
- "Improved sharing — A short new https://maps.apple URL makes sharing places and Guides easier."

### Music
- "AutoMix — Like a DJ, AutoMix seamlessly transitions from one song into the next, using time stretching and beat matching."
- "Music Pins — Elevate your most-loved music to the top of your library."
- "Lyrics Translation and Pronunciation — Understand the lyrics and sing along in different languages, right from the Now Playing view."

### Accessibility
- "Accessibility Reader — A new, systemwide reading mode is designed to make text easier to read for users who are blind, have low vision, or have other disabilities affecting reading."
- "Braille Access — A powerful new way for Braille users with connected displays to interact with their Apple devices."
- "Faster and easier Personal Voice creation."
- "Customize Vehicle Motion Cues."
- "Name Recognition."
- "Live Captions for Live Listen."
- "Share Accessibility Settings."
- "Head Tracking."
- "Accessibility Nutrition Labels — Learn about accessibility features for apps and games in the App Store."

### Reminders / Notes / Productivity
- "Suggested Reminders — With Apple Intelligence, Reminders can now review an email, website, note, or other text on your device to find the most relevant tasks or grocery items."
- "Quick Reminders — Quickly create reminders on your Home Screen by pressing your iPhone Action button or tapping in Control Center."
- "Auto-categorize — Automatically categorize your reminders into tidy sections within a list."
- "Time Zone Support — Individual reminders can have a time zone selected for them."
- "Reed pen — Available in the tool palette, this new pen uses stroke angle presets to enable a calligraphy experience."

### Wallet / Gaming / Sports / Settings
- Wallet Digital ID, pay with installments, order tracking, updated boarding passes, pay with rewards.
- Game Overlay, Gaming Hub, Game Library, Challenges, Apple Arcade, controller support, MetalFX.
- Apple Sports widget.
- Settings: Centralized Blocked List, Adaptive Power, Estimated Time to Charge, New Battery UI.

## Excerpts (macOS 26 release notes)

- Foundation Models framework: direct access to the on-device LLM at the core of Apple Intelligence.
- Accessibility Nutrition Labels in App Store product pages.
- Game Controller: Home button opens Game Overlay; multi-device DualSense pairing.
- Metal 4, MetalFX Frame Interpolation / Denoising.
- Swift Charts 3D (`Chart3D`).
- SwiftUI button sizing / bordered tint in apps adopting the new design.
- WebKit `WebPage` navigations async sequence.
- Maps route planning fixes; SwiftUI Map tap gesture known issues.
- Messages resolved/known issues (sent translated messages, screen unknown senders defaults, conversation details).
- Photos picker search crash workaround.
- Safari cache workaround for facebook.com.
